What to find in this facility?

A diverssified collection of technical and vocaltional resources with reference to over 250 promulgated Training Regulatioins of TESDA. Other materials in business, arts, , sciences, sociial media and health are also availabe to all clients.

How to access the collections?

The fresources can be accessed physically or by using electronic devices such as computers, tablets and smart phones. This facillity is open eight hours a day from Monday to Friday only. Visitors are required to register to be given access.

What's on your mind?

Before leaving the facility, our staff will ask you to fill ups an evaluation form to help improve uour services.

 

What's new:

Acquisitions of new e-resources and journals

acquisition of new materials will encourage ATVRC clients to access information online via in-house computers or by using personal gadgets such as laptop computers, tablets and smart phones.
